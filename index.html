About Us

Welcome to Ecostyle Couture Limited, where style meets sustainability.

At Ecostyle Couture, we are passionate about creating garments and accessories that combine timeless elegance with an eco-friendly touch. Founded by Jeffrey Akuchu Tetteh, our mission is to redefine the fashion industry by promoting sustainability without compromising on quality or style.

Who We Are

Ecostyle Couture Limited is a full-service fashion and sewing company dedicated to crafting high-quality garments for a variety of needs. From custom-tailored suits and wedding gowns to corporate and school uniforms, we cater to individuals and organizations looking for exceptional craftsmanship. In addition, our innovative bag-making services reflect our commitment to functional yet stylish designs, incorporating eco-friendly materials whenever possible.

Our Vision

To become a leading name in the sustainable fashion industry by delivering exceptional products and services that inspire confidence and reflect care for the environment.

Our Mission

  • To provide high-quality, tailor-made garments and accessories that meet the unique needs of our clients.
  • To champion sustainability by incorporating eco-friendly practices into every aspect of our work.
  • To ensure customer satisfaction through innovation, attention to detail, and excellent service.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Sustainability: We believe in protecting the environment while staying fashionable. Our designs integrate sustainable practices and eco-friendly materials.
  • Quality Craftsmanship: Every piece we create is crafted with precision and care to ensure the highest quality.
  • Custom Designs: We understand that no two clients are the same. That’s why we offer personalized designs tailored to fit your style and preferences.

Join us on this journey where sustainability meets style.
